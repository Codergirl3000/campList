How to start a fire:

1.get matches and fire wood, other optinal stuff to bring is dry grass and rocks.
2.make a tipi` whith your wood.
3.put rocks if you brought them around your tipi
4.use the match to light the middle of your tipi.
5.now let your fire build up
6.last but not least ENJOY YOUR FIRE!

How make the perfect smore with your fire:

1.you need marshmellows, gram crackers, choclolet bars,
and a rosting stick(if you do not have one you can use: a normal
stick, a chopstick, or a skewer)
2.put your marshmellow on your stick
3.hold 2 inches above fire
4.slowly turn it clockwise until you hit 12 then
turn counter clockwise until you hit 12 again
5.once the marshmellow is golden brown put your marshmellow on the gram cracker with the choclolet bars
6.ENJOY YOUR S'MORES
